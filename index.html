Goalkeeper's Uniform & Gloves — 한승주 추가 프린트 ④
2학년 반 번
이름:

★ 다음 글을 읽고, 물음에 답해 봅시다.

As a goalkeeper, my uniform is really important. ⓐ It's not just my clothes to wear in the game — ⓑ it helps me on the field. My uniform color is different from the other players on the team. And my uniform is usually a bright color, so my teammates and fans can spot me easily, especially when the game is moving fast.

My gloves are also important. Only goalkeepers can use their hands during soccer games, so I must wear gloves. I can catch the ball, block shots, and make big saves thanks to ⓒ them. Goalkeeper gloves didn't even exist before! However, ⓓ they started to wear gloves to protect their hands because the game got faster and tougher. Today, goalkeeper gloves are made of special materials, so goalkeepers can catch the ball more easily. ⓔ They're like a second skin to me. In any situation during the game, my gloves and uniform help me.
